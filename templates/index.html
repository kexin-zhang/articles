How It Works

Explore the news by using the search bar to look up topics. You'll see the volume of artices about the topic each day, as well as keywords commonly used in articles about that topic.


Don't know what to search? Try 'America', 'Election', or 'Obama'.

About

For a week, I scraped news articles from 12 sources:

  • CNN
  • The New York Times
  • The Huffington Post
  • The Guardian
  • Fox News
  • BBC News
  • Yahoo News
  • USA Today
  • The Atlantic
  • The Washington Post
  • The Wall Street Journal
  • Reuters

Article scraping and keyword extraction was done with Python's Newspaper library.


The topics and keywords in those articles are visualized on this site, with the help of MaterializeCSS, D3.js, and MetricGraphics.js.
